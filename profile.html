Bridging Software Engineering with Next-Gen Connectivity.

My Journey

I am a Computer Science Engineering student with a passion for understanding how massive systems communicate. My path took a major turn when I completed a 16-week 5G Communication & Technology certification (Cultus/TNSDC), which opened my eyes to the world of disaggregated RAN and Core networks.


I believe that technology is a tool for social good. Whether I am deploying a private 5G core or building a Travel Portal for the Disabled, my goal is to create systems that are reliable, high-performing, and inclusive.

Technical Values

I am a firm believer in the Ubuntu Linux ecosystem. I prefer working in command-line environments where I can manage configurations directly. I am currently deepening my knowledge in Cloud-Native systems to understand the future of IoT and C-V2X.

Interests

Outside of networking, I focus on Web Accessibility (WCAG). I want to ensure the future of the web is accessible to everyone, regardless of their physical abilities.
